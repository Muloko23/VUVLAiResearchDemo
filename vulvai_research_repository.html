The repository
We start with pelvic conditions. We scale to all of women's gynecologic health.
The first condition — vestibulodynia — is the proof of concept. It proves that VULVAi's three-vertical model can capture structured, longitudinal, research-grade data from symptoms to diagnosis to outcomes. Every subsequent condition adds a new layer to the same infrastructure.

VULVAiResearch capabilities
At scale
What this enables
From a diagnostic tool to a research platform for all of women's health.
Once the data infrastructure exists, it unlocks capabilities that are impossible without it — large-scale questionnaires, population-level databases, real-world evidence studies, and eventually the clinical trials that women have been excluded from for decades.
Research capabilities unlocked by the repository
DB
Large-scale longitudinal databases
The first structured, consent-driven, longitudinal database linking symptoms, hormones, confirmed diagnoses, and treatment outcomes in women's gynecologic health. Built from primary data — not EHR abstraction or insurance claims.
Q
Population-scale questionnaires
Embedded research surveys across 847+ active patients — scaling to millions as the platform grows. Every questionnaire is standardized, validated, and linked to clinical outcomes. No recruitment cost. No response bias from self-selected research populations.
RWE
Real-world evidence studies
Pharma, medical device, and biotech companies need real-world evidence on how women respond to treatments. VULVAi's longitudinal dataset — with confirmed diagnoses, hormone panels, and treatment outcomes — is exactly this. No dataset like it exists.
↺
Pragmatic clinical trials
With an existing consented patient population, confirmed diagnoses, and longitudinal tracking already in place — VULVAi can run pragmatic trials at a fraction of traditional cost. The recruitment problem is solved before the trial begins.

The vision
VULVAi is not just a diagnostic tool.
It is the research backbone of women's gynecologic health.
Every condition we add, every patient we enroll, every physician we train makes the dataset more powerful. The infrastructure we're building today will support the clinical trials, population databases, and real-world evidence studies that women's health has never had — and desperately needs.
The compound effect
More patients → better data → stronger science → better care → more patients
This is not a product with a research feature. This is a research platform that happens to deliver direct value to patients and physicians along the way. Every interaction — a patient logging her symptoms, a doctor confirming a diagnosis, a resident answering a quiz question — is a data point entering the most comprehensive longitudinal database in women's gynecologic health. No competitor can buy this. It has to be built. And we are building it.
